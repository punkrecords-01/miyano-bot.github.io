Fintech Real Estate Strategy

DIGITAL
MORTGAGE

End-to-end UX strategy for a complex financial product — transforming ambiguity into clear, scalable solutions.

Role UX Designer & Strategist
Duration 8 Months
Context Fintech / Real Estate

01 / THE PROBLEM

Opaque &
Bureaucratic

Home financing was perceived as slow and complex. Users frequently abandoned the process due to unclear requirements and lack of status visibility. Internally, disconnected teams and manual handoffs caused delays, rework, and inconsistent decisions.

// Impact Before Design

High Drop-off during application

Long approval cycles

Cross-team operational inefficiency

02 / OWNERSHIP

The UX
Strategist

I led the UX strategy end-to-end, acting as the bridge between user needs and business constraints in a regulated environment.

// Responsibilities

  • Research synthesis and problem framing
  • Experience strategy and journey definition
  • User flow mapping and service blueprinting
  • Information architecture and interaction logic
  • Alignment with product, engineering, legal, & ops

// Strategy Approach

  • — Clarity over completeness
  • — Visibility over control
  • — Reduce cognitive load, not just steps

03 / CORE ARTIFACTS

Evidence &
Outcomes

User Flow Map

Mapped the full journey to expose redundant validations, unnecessary decision points, and moments of user uncertainty.

Outcome: Simplified experience and removed friction that caused drop-off.

Service Blueprint

Connected frontstage user actions with backstage operations (credit analysis, legal checks, internal approvals).

Outcome: Reduced misalignment between teams and minimized rework.

04 / DESIGN DECISIONS

Balanced
Solutions

Process Transparency

Prioritized understanding over speed illusions, ensuring users always knew their status.

Value-Driven Consolidation

Consolidated steps that created no user or business value to streamline the flow.

Status Feedback Loop

Introduced clear, proactive status updates instead of silent, anxiety-inducing waiting periods.

Compliance Management

Designed for regulatory constraints without exposing complexity to the end-users.

"Users didn’t mind waiting for credit analysis — they minded not knowing what was happening."

— Key Project Insight
